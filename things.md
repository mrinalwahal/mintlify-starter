1. [Casio Youth F-91WB-1ADF Black Watch](https://www.casio.com/in/watches/casio/product.F-91WB-1A/) - ~Rs. 1,500 in July, 2025

    I bought it for ~ Rs. 1,500. It's simple, cheap, minimal, black, has a nearly 7 year long battery life, and is water resistant. I have even gone swimming wearing it. It was also worn both by Osama Bin Laden and Barack Obama, because of how accurate it is. Looks great with any outfit. I have been wearing it nearly everyday since I bought it.

1. Borosil Hydra Coffeemate (350ml) - ~Rs. 300 in 2025

    I use it nearly every day for my Americano and have taken it to everywhere with me, including on treks, vacations, events, long drives, etc. Keeps my drink hot or cold for a lot of hours. It's durable, easy to clean, ergonomic and fits perfectly in my car's cup holder. I can throw it around anywhere without worrying about it breaking or leaking my drink.

1. iPhone 14 - ~Rs. 70,000 in October, 2023

1. Apple Mac Air M1 - ~Rs. 93,000 in June, 2021

    I have been using it every single day since I bought it. It's smooth, durable, well-built, never hangs, has a decent battery, and is very reliable. I use it for coding, writing, streaming and just about everything. It's also very lightweight and portable, making it easy to carry around. I haven't had any issues with it so far. I am likely going to use it for at least 10 complete years.

1. [Decathlon Beige Cargo Pant (NH500)](https://www.decathlon.in/p/968672) - ~ Rs 1,400 in 2023

    I have worn this trouser literally everywhere - treks, vacations, events, long drives, etc. It's comfortable, durable, stretchable, has a lot of pockets and is very versatile. I can pair it with any shirt and it looks good. I liked it so much that I bought a second one in 2025. If you have met me more than once, you have probably seen me wearing it.

1. [Decathlon Grey Sports Sandals (NH500)](https://www.decathlon.in/p/8357753) - Rs. 2,000 in 2025

    I bought these to save myself the time of wearing and removing my socks every time I wanted to go out for a quick coffee run or anywhere I wouldn't need to walk more than a kilometer. They are very comfortable, durable, and look good with any outfit.

1. [Logitech MX Master Keys (Mini) Keyboard](https://amzn.in/d/02ui0f4Q) - Got it for free in 2022

    I have been using it every single day since I got it. It's very ergonomic, smooth, durable, well-built, has a decent battery, and is very reliable. I have even dropped it a few times and it never broke. Never even needed servicing. It gives me the exact same typing experience as my Mac Air's keyboard. It doesn't have mechanical keys, but I kinda prefer it that way. I don't think I've ever used a better keyboard than this one. If this fails someday, I'll probably repeat this purchase.

1. [Decathlong Black Jacket](https://www.decathlon.in/p/8370601) - ~ Rs. 800 in 2022

    I purchased this jacket on a cold wintery night before reaching a friend's place for his birthday party. I didn't think twice before buying it because I just needed some protection. It's been a great purchase. It's very warm, comfortable, durable, and looks good with any outfit. I have worn it on treks, at home, on vacations, at events, long drives, etc. I have even worn it to work a few times. I wear it nearly every day during winters and it still looks as good as new. I am likely going to use it for a lot of years to come.
